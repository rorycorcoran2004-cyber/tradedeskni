Questions

Common questions.

What information do I need to send?
Customer name, address, what you did, what you're charging. A voice note works fine — most clients send one on the drive home and the document is waiting when they get back.
How fast do I actually get the document?
Around 17 seconds on average. The system runs continuously so documents come back at any time of day or night.
Will it look like I produced it myself?
Yes. Every document goes out with your business name, contact details and registration number. Your customers won't know we're involved.
What trades do you cover?
Plumbers, gas engineers, electricians, roofers, plasterers, painters and decorators, joiners — any trade that generates quotes, invoices and compliance paperwork.
What happens after the free trial?
After 2 weeks it's £79–£249/month in NI or €79–€249/month in the Republic depending on your plan. No contract — cancel any time with a single message.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. No contract, no notice period. Send a message and you're done. Your documents stay in your portal for 90 days after cancellation.
